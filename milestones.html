Research Project Milestones

Project Assessments

Project Proposal


Project proposal is an informal "Contract" to establish an agreement about the contents and limits of the final output. Every group had to submit their proposal reports (4 Individual and 1 Group) to the Cloud Drive link provided by the RP team. Four individual reports to explain the individual components and one group document to explain the proposal for the entire system, and to emphasize how the system will be developed within the year to achieve the final outcome.

Submission Date: 25th February 2020
Allocated Marks: 10%

Progress Presentation 01


PP1 was conducted as an online assessment during 13th & 14th of July 2020 via Microsoft Teams with the group members and the panel members to evaluate the 50% of the progress. A total of 25 mins was allocated for the assessment (20 mins for the Presentation and 5 mins for the Q&A Session). Some students had to change their scope through the supervisors, and these students were evaluated based on the new scope submitted by the supervisor.

Submission Date: 13th July 2020
Allocated Marks: 10%

Progress Presentation 02


An on-campus presentation was conducted in order to evaluate the 90% of the progress. Displaying a Camera-ready poster was also required in the presentation. A total of 35 mins was allocated for the assessment, and each student received 5 mins to present their progress. Also, another 10 mins were given for the product demonstration. The next 5 mins were used by the Examining panel for the Q&A session and to review the poster.

Submission Date: 26th September 2020
Allocated Marks: 18%

Demo


A camera-ready poster which was prepared in order to provide a better understanding of the application; the appearance of the application,how it works, what methods were used in implementing the application etc.

Submission Date: 23th September 2020
Allocated Marks: 10%

Final Assessment


The final assessment includes submitting the final report and doing the final presentation. For the final report 15% of the marks will be given while 10% is given for the presentation. In this stage the research should be completed.

Submission Date: 26th September 2020
Allocated Marks: 25%.

Viva


A viva will be held to evaluate the individual performance and contribution for the research. Marks will be given accordingly for each member after evaluating their work and presentation skills.

Submission Date: 15th November 2020
Allocated Marks: 15%
